Oreo Pizza

Description

Cookies and Cream dessert is always a go to dessert after eating a dish, this delicious dessert is easy and yummy to make and will satisfy your sweet tooth

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chip Cookie dough
  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Oreos
  • White Chocolate Chip

Step by Step

  1. To make the dessert pizza start by preheating your oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. As it heats, prepare your cookie dough by pressing it onto 12” or larger round pizza pan or cookie sheet in the shape of circle.
  3. If you use a larger pan, aim for about a 12” diameter for your dough.
  4. Place the dough into the oven and bake for about 10 to 15 minutes or until slightly golden brown.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool.
  6. As it bakes, mix together the cream cheese, butter, vanilla extract and powdered sugar in a medium bowl until combined.
  7. Spread the mixture over the cooled cookie crust.
  8. Sprinkle the top with chopped Oreos until you cover the whole pizza.
  9. Add candy melts to a small piping bag and melt.
  10. Once they are melted, cut the tip off and drizzle over the pizza.
  11. Cut it and now Enjoy!
