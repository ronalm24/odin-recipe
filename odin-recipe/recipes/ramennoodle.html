Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

This Chicken Ramen Noodle Soup is the easiest and most tasty Noodle soup you'll ever try and eat.

What You'll Need

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced carrot
  • ¾ cup sliced celery
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 2 packages ramen noodles with seasoning packet
  • ¼ teaspoon lime zest, or to taste

Step By Step

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add carrot, celery, and onion. Cook until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Add ginger and garlic. Cook 1 minute more. Add broth, soy sauce, basil, oregano, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until carrots are just tender about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in cooked chicken and ramen noodles. Simmer, uncovered, until noodles are tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in lime juice(optional). Ladle soup into bowls. Sprinkle with lime zest and, if you like, additional basil and oregano leaves, and serve with Honey-Sriracha Crackers.
